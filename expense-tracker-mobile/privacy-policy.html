Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 8, 2026

Expense Tracker ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal finance management application that helps users track income, expenses, budgets, and recurring transactions. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the app's functionality:

  • Account information: Username, email address, and password (hashed, never stored in plain text).
  • Financial data: Transaction amounts, categories, descriptions, and dates that you manually enter into the app.
  • Budget data: Budget limits and categories you configure.
  • Recurring transaction data: Frequency, amounts, and schedules you set up.

We do not collect:

  • Bank account numbers, credit card numbers, or any real financial account credentials
  • Location data
  • Contact lists or phone data
  • Device identifiers for advertising purposes
  • Data from other apps on your device

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide expense tracking, budgeting, and reporting features
  • To send email notifications you have opted into (budget alerts, weekly summaries, password reset codes)
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored on secure servers hosted by Railway using PostgreSQL databases.
  • All data transmission between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using encrypted storage (Expo SecureStore).
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms (PBKDF2 via Django) and are never stored in plain text.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. All financial data you enter remains associated with your account until you choose to delete individual records or request account deletion.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your financial data at any time within the app.
  • Modify: Edit or delete any transaction, budget, or recurring transaction you have created.
  • Delete: Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us.
  • Data portability: Your data is accessible through the app and can be exported upon request.

6. Email Communications

We may send you transactional emails including password reset codes, large expense alerts, budget threshold notifications, and weekly spending summaries. You can manage your notification preferences in the app's Profile settings.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services for infrastructure only (not for tracking or advertising):

  • Railway: Cloud hosting and database services
  • Google Gmail SMTP: Sending transactional emails (password resets, alerts)
  • Expo / EAS: App build and update infrastructure

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
